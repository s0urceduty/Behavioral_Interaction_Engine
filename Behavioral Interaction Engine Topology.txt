Behavioral Interaction Engine (BIE)
│
├── Core Algebraic Model
│   └── S = f(α * C + β * K)
│       ├── C: Cooperation dimension
│       ├── K: Competition dimension
│       ├── α: Weight for cooperation
│       ├── β: Weight for competition
│       └── f: Non-linear function capturing system dynamics
│
├── Agent-Level Specification
│   ├── Agent characteristics
│   ├── Preference encoding (α, β)
│   └── Behavioral input values (C, K)
│
├── System-Level Dynamics
│   ├── Aggregated outcomes (S across agents)
│   ├── Pattern recognition (e.g., oscillations, equilibria)
│   └── Emergent properties
│
├── Feedback Mechanisms
│   ├── Temporal adjustment of α, β, C, K
│   ├── Learning and adaptation loops
│   └── Environmental influence integration
│
└── Domain Applications
    ├── Economics: collusion vs. rivalry
    ├── Ecology: symbiosis vs. competition
    ├── Social networks: collaboration vs. status competition
    └── Others: customizable domain modeling
